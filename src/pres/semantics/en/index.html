Content

  • Language: <html lang="en">, <html lang="ru">
  • Headings: <h1>, <h2>, <h3>, <h4>, <h5>, <h6>
  • Images: <img alt="">, <svg role="image" aria-label="">
  • Figures: <figure>, <figcaption>
  • Lists: <ul>, <ol>, <li>

Interface?

Interface

  • Landmarks: <header>, <main>, <aside>, <footer>
  • Headings: <h1>, <h2>, <h3>, <h4>, <h5>, <h6>
  • Form elements: <fieldset>, <legend>, <input>
  • Interactive elements: <button> and <a>
  • Labels: <a href="" aria-label="Twitter"></a>

For keyboard and screen reader users.
